How to encrypt

  1. Enter your secret message in the form
  2. If you want to encrypt the files, download the desktop version
  3. Press the "Encipher It" button
  4. Enter a password to encrypt your message. Please use long passwords, for example, a few random words.
  5. The browser encrypts your message with 256-bit AES encryption on your side, without sending us your original text or your password
  6. Optionally, save the encrypted message or file to an online file storage and get a short link to access it online or share it
  7. If needed, provide the password to other party via a safe communication channel.

How to decrypt

  1. When you receive encrypted text or open short link, do one of the following:
    • Go to https://encipher.it and paste the message (or just click on the short link)
    • Use the bookmarklet or download the Chrome extension to decrypt the message in Gmail or other webmail
    • Download the desktop version to decrypt the files
  2. Press the "Decipher It" button
  3. Enter the password
  4. Well done, you have successfully gained access to the secret message or files.
