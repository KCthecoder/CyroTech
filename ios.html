  • How To Add Bookmarklet

Encipher.it bookmarklet is a snippet of code that you can store in your browser bookmarks to encrypt email, or any block of text you enter on a website. Visit site for the video tutorial.

Adding bookmarklet is a little bit tricky on apple devices, due to iOS limitations. To do this, please follow the next steps:

Tap here - this will change page title and append bookmarklet into address bar

Select Add Bookmark

Tap Save

Tap the Bookmarks button

Locate just created Encipher.It bookmark in the list.

Tap Edit and then tap on the bookmark

Tap in the second field (it begins with 'http').You want to edit the beginning of this text, so tap and drag the loupe all the way to the left.

Place the editing point between the "#" and the "j" then delete all to the beginning of the line

When you are done, your bookmark should begin from 'javascript' string. Save and use!
